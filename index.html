My Best Food is Plantain and Fried Egg

    Ingredient:
  • Red pepper
  • Groundnut oil
  • Ripe plantain
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Salt

Procedure for Making Fried Plantain:



  • Peel the skin of the plantains by cutting off the top and bottom of the plantains then cut a horizontal line on the yellow peel to enable you easily remove it.
  • After removing all the plantain peels slice the plantain into a bowl and add a pinch of salt.


  • Put frying pan on the gas cooker and add groundnut oil, leave it for some minutes to allow it heat up briefly.
  • Add the sliced into the hot oil and fry on medium heat.
  • Turn the plantain slightly to prevent it from getting burnt
  • Then serve the plantain

Procedure for Making Fried Egg:

  • Blend the tomatoes, peppers and onions to a rough texture.
  • Put small amount of groundnut oil in the frying pan
  • Place frying pan on the gas coooker to heat the oil
  • Add the blended tomatoes, onions and pepper mix
  • Add salt, mix well and allow it to fry
  • Crack egg into a bowl and whisk it
  • Pour the eggs into the freid pepper sauce and mix it well
  • Fry on low heat while mixing at different intervals to achieve properly cooked scrambled eggs.
    The plantain and egg is ready to serve
